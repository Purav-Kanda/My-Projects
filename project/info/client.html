4. Project Overview

This web app aims to provide a professional and interactive platform showcasing Kevin Yang’s skills, interests, achievements, and hobbies. The client requires a solution that effectively presents his personal and professional information while maintaining a modern and user-friendly design. The webpage should also include interactive elements, such as mini-games, to enhance user engagement. Additionally, the page must be mobile-responsive to ensure an optimal user experience across different devices.


5. Target Users

Primary Users: Students, potential employers, colleagues, and general visitors.

Device Preference: Mobile, iPad, and desktop.

Special Needs or Considerations: The design must be flexible, modern, and user-friendly, ensuring seamless navigation and engagement.

7. Design Preferences

  • Preferred Look & Feel: Professional and corporate-style layout.
  • Color Scheme & Branding: A sleek, modern, and professional design that aligns with industry standards.
  • Elements to Avoid: No distracting advertisements; unnecessary text should be minimized to keep the page clean and focused.

8. Data Management & Presentation

  • Professional Showcase: Information on Kevin Yang’s skills, achievements, and interests.
  • Visual Content: Pictures from social media accounts, resume details, and workshops attended.
  • Content Management: A dedicated section for visitor interactions, such as a review or reach-out page.

9. Interactive Features (TBDs)
